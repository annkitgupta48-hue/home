Frequently Asked Questions

Is Job Spare free to use?

Yes, searching and applying for jobs is completely free for candidates. Employers can also post basic job listings for free.

How do I apply for a job?

Click on any job card to view details, then click the "Apply Now" button. This will usually take you to the company's official application page.

Can I post a job without an account?

No, you must create an account to post a job. This helps us maintain the quality and authenticity of job listings.

How long does it take for my job to be approved?

Most jobs are reviewed within 24 hours. You can check the status of your job post in your dashboard.
